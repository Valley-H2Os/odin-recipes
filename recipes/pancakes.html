Pancakes

Description

Easy and delicious breakfast treat that does not require much thought when tired and groggy in the morning. Toppings can include: syrup, jelly, whipped cream, bananas, strawberries, and more!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Pour in milk, add egg and oil. Continue mixing until smooth.
  3. Heat frying pan to medium. Lightly oil pan with vegetable oil.
  4. Pour or scoop portion of batter into the frying pan. Cook on one side until edges brown, then flip. Cook on side until both sides of the pancake are golden brown.
  5. Repeat process until all batter has been cooked. Serve with desired toppings. Enjoy!
