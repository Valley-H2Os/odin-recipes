Muddy Buddies

Description

Muddies Buddies (also known as Puppy Chow, Monkey Munch, or Doggy Bag) are a quick and easy to make homemade cereal candy. This American sweet treat is a must-have for the holidays! Muddy Buddies are a finger-food and made to be eaten without milk. Perfect for any occassion!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 9 cups crispy rice cereal squares
  • 1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Set stove to low heat and melt the chocolate chips, stirring until smooth.

  2. Step 2

    3. Add in peanut butter and stir until smooth.

  3. Step 3

    4. Remove from heat. Stir in cereal squares and mix until completely coated.

  4. Step 4

    5. Pour powdered sugar into a large, airtight container or plastic bag. Add cereal and mix/shake until coated.

  5. Step 5

    6. Enjoy!
