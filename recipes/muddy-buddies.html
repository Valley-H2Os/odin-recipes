Muddy Buddies

Description

Muddies Buddies (also known as Puppy Chow, Monkey Munch, or Doggy Bag) are a quick and easy to make homemade cereal candy. This American sweet treat is a must-have for the holidays! Muddy Buddies are a finger-food and made to be eaten without milk. Perfect for any occassion!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 9 cups crispy rice cereal squares
  • 1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Set stove to low heat and melt the chocolate chips, stirring until smooth.
  2. Add in peanut butter and stir until smooth.
  3. Remove from heat. Stir in cereal squares and mix until completely coated.
  4. Pour powdered sugar into a large, airtight container or plastic bag. Add cereal and mix/shake until coated.
  5. Enjoy!
